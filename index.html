about us
We offer a curated selection of healthy natural foods and beverages.from local and international brands that meet all your health, dietary, and lifestyle needs and delivered to you all over Lebanon.

Selection Process

Unlike your typical “health” store, we have a very strict selection process for food, drink, personal care, cleaning, and wellness products.  Our process is based on an ever-growing list of banned ingredients that we update regularly. So, you can feel confident in what you purchase from us. If it doesn’t meet our standards, we don’t sell it. 

Food & Drink

We ban over 200 ingredients from our store including preservatives (citric acid, cultured dextrose), artificial flavors, artificial colors, artificial sweeteners (acesulfame-k, advantame, aspartame, saccharin, sucralose), bleached flours, high-fructose corn syrup, hydrogenated and partially hydrogenated oils, refined vegetable oils, refined sugars, and much more... 

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order for healthy products on good bites?

+

Ordering from good bites is simple and convenient. Browse our extensive range of healthy foods and drinks, add your desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can easily filter products by dietary needs or preferences, ensuring you find exactly what suits your lifestyle.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept a variety of secure payment options, including credit and debit cards,We currently accept cash on delivery in USD and in LBP based on the delivery date's rate. We also accept money transfer with OMT / Wish / Western Union. Your financial information is protected through our secure payment processing system, allowing you to shop with confidence.

Can I track my order after I've placed it?

+

Absolutely! Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to follow your package as it makes its way to you, ensuring you're always informed about your delivery status.

Do you offer returns or exchanges for your products?

+

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your items, our return policy allows you to initiate a return within a specified period. Please check our Shipping & Returns page for more detailed information on the process.

How can I contact customer service for assistance?

+

Our customer service team is here to help! You can reach out to us through our Contact Us page, where you'll find a contact form and our phone number. We're dedicated to providing you with prompt and helpful support.

Can I Modify My Order After Placing It?

+

If you need to make changes to your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. We’ll do our best to accommodate your requests before the order is processed.

What Should I Do If I Receive a Damaged Product?

+

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service. If you receive a damaged product, please reach out to our customer support team within 48 hours of delivery. We will assist you with replacements or refunds to ensure your satisfaction.
